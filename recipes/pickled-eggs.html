Pickled Eggs

Image pulled from Wikipedia

Description

Picked Eggs are hard boiled eggs cured in brine and vinegar. Originally a way to store eggs over winter, Pickled Eggs have become a favorite in bars and as snacks.

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • Mason jar
  • 2 cups Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Hard boil and peel the eggs. It's best to do this with a tablespoon of vinegar to soften the shells while cooking.
  2. cut onion into rings, crush the garlic cloves. Put them in a pot with the vinegar, salt, and water. Simmer until the onions become soft and translucent
  3. Add the fluid to the mason jar. Add in peeled eggs and onions/garlic in layers, until the jar is as stuffed as possible
  4. Chill in the fridge for at least a week. Eggs should stay good for 3 months.
