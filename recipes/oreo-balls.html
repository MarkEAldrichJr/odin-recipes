Oreo Balls

Image pulled from Wikimedia Commons

Description

A personal favorite holiday treat! It's okay if you don't like them, I just won't be able to associate with you out of principle.

Also note the recipe is not super exact. Measure the indredients with your whole heart.

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of oreos
  • 3-4 8oz softened cream cheeses
  • melting chocolate

Instructions

  1. Grind Oreos to a paste
  2. Mix oreos with softened cream cheese until homogenous
  3. Make into balls, put in freezer
  4. Melt melting chocolate in a double boiler
  5. When chocolate is melted and the balls are mostly frozen, pull them out of the fridge, and dunk and toss them in the chocolate 2 or 3 at a time, put them on a waxpaper sheet.
  6. Once all the balls are covered, put them in the fridge until the chocolate hardens.
