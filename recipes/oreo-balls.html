Switchel

Image pulled from trailcooking.com

Description

Switchel is a cold brew drink sharing common roots to shrub and kombucha, differing in the chosen sweetener. While most shrub drinks use fruit pastes, Swichel uses molasses, honey, or maple syrup! It is a common drink in the South for farm workers and day laborers.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 gallons Water
  • 2 cups Molasses
  • 1 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoons Ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Grind ginger.
  2. Mix molasses, vinegar, ginger, and salt, stir until homogenous.
  3. Mix with water. Again, mix until homogenous
  4. Chill in the fridge overnight.
